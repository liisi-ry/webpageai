BEST-Estonia

BEST-Estonia is part of the organization BEST (Board of European Students of Technology), which unites students from different technical universities across Europe.

History

A short overview of BEST history

1989
Official founding of BEST in Berlin, Germany
1990–1991
First Summer Programme (Kursus Suvel) and international student exchange
1991
Founding of BEST-Estonia in TalTech
1996
XIV General Assembly in Tallinn, founding of the H3E Thematic Network
2009
Launch of the European BEST Engineering Competition (EBEC)
2013–2015
The launch of BEST Career Day (BCD)
2025
10 departments that connect 29 countries over Europe

Events and Projects

BEST members can participate in and contribute to various events. Our largest projects include Võti Tulevikku, a career fair we have organized for over 30 years; Enginaator, an engineering competition held since 2018; Kursus Suvel (Summer Programme), which brings students from other European countries to Estonia; and UMW (Uute Motivational Weekend), which welcomes our newest members to BEST.

Why join BEST-Estonia?

Develop valuable professional skills

By joining BEST-Estonia, you can grow your abilities in sales, marketing, design, IT, and project management. We focus on developing these skills through trainings, workshops, and real hands-on experience in organizing events and initiatives.

Be part of an active and supportive community

BEST-Estonia brings together motivated and friendly students who make teamwork both productive and fun. You’ll meet great people, make lasting friendships, and enjoy social activities alongside meaningful projects.

Experience international opportunities

As part of the international BEST network, members can participate in week-long trainings, courses, and cultural events across Europe, meeting students from different countries and broadening their horizons beyond TalTech.
